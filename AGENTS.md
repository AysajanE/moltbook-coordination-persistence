# AGENTS.md

This repo is the standalone flagship-paper environment. Scientific rigor and provenance are the governing standard.

- Do not import old data, old result tables, old figures, old discussion text, or old conclusions from the parent repo.
- Stage 3 theory and methods are locked; fresh empirical evidence must be generated in this repo.
- Do not fabricate results, file contents, data provenance, or manuscript claims.
- Keep edits narrow and additive. Prefer manifests and decision logs over silent replacements.
- Do not commit raw data. Keep `data_raw/`, `data_curated/`, `data_features/`, and `outputs/` out of git.
- If a script or document is not clearly still aligned to the new flagship design, leave it out rather than porting it forward.
